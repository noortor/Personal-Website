About Me

Here you can learn more about who I am and what I do :D

Career

My name is Noor and I am a second year studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at UC Berkeley. I was an intern at Amazon on the Paragon team as a part of their Amazon Future Engineer Program and am excited to return to the company next summer as a Software Development Engineering intern. I have full stack experience and I hope to gain the opportunity to share my experience and knowledge with other students.

Teaching

One thing I love to do is teach others, whether they be my peers or younger students who need help in STEM subjects. I am a tutor for Youth Tennis Advantage, a local organization that provides tutoring and tennis coaching for under-resourced students in Berkeley and Oakland, CA. I have been a volunteer for them since I was a freshman in high school. Last summer, I volunteered as a Calculus tutor for Blossom Tutoring, a student-lead organization that provides tutoring for high school students facing academically-inhibiting challenges due to the COVID-19 pandemic. I am also an academic intern for CS61B, the data structures course at UC Berkeley.

Hobbies

In my free time, I like to play viola, piano, tennis, and video games. I played viola in middle and high schools, as well as in other various ensembles. I play piano as a way to relax and wind down after a long day of work. Ever since I was a kid, I have always tried to stay active with tennis. I played competitively in tournaments when I was younger, but now coach and play casually with friends and family.
